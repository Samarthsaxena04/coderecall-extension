# 🧩 CodeRecall — Browser Extension

> Track DSA problems and manage your revision schedule without ever leaving the problem page.

## ✨ What it does

When you open any problem on LeetCode, Codeforces, GeeksforGeeks, or other supported platforms — just click the CodeRecall extension icon. It automatically:

- ✅ **Detects the problem title, URL, and platform**
- 🏷️ **Scrapes topic tags** (Array, DP, Binary Search, etc.)
- 💾 **Saves to your CodeRecall profile** with one click
- 📅 **Shows questions due for revision today** using spaced repetition
- 🔁 **Mark revisions done** without going to the website

---

## 📦 Installation

> **Requires Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Opera** (any Chromium-based browser)

### Step 1 — Download

👉 [Download coderecall-extension.zip](https://github.com/Samarthsaxena04/coderecall-extension/releases/latest/download/coderecall-extension.zip)

### Step 2 — Unzip

Extract the zip to a folder on your computer. Remember the location.

- **Windows:** Right-click → *Extract All*
- **Mac:** Double-click the zip

### Step 3 — Open Extensions Page

Type this in your browser address bar and press Enter:

### Step 4 — Enable Developer Mode

Toggle **Developer Mode** ON (top-right corner of the extensions page).

### Step 5 — Load the Extension

Click **"Load Unpacked"** → select the unzipped **`chrome-extension`** folder.

### Step 6 — Sign In

Click the CodeRecall icon in your toolbar → sign in with your account → done! 🎉
